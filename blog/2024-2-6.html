A good idea!? Teach 100… No, 50? Elderlies to use AI?

Yesterday after the monthly desperate discussion on the same old topic, activities, my parents and I decided that I should work based on my past research paper on the digital divide and the elderly. AI is added as a new element given my interest in it. So the plan is to train a AI model particularly good at helping elderlies and teach some random old people to use this model. The former is quite easy with the help of Baidu Agent Builder, which I was playing around recently. The latter is what’s challenging. Some questions remain unanswered.

Q: How many elderlies do I wanna teach?
A: 100 would be great because it sounds nice but it’s a bit unrealistic and maybe I should start with 10.

Q: I what ways can the project be presented?
A: Website can be a great choice, along with public accounts or even videos.

Q: What do you want to know from the elderlies?
A: Haven’t decided yet, maybe their occupations, their needs, their age…

Q: What name should the project have?
A: Haven’t decided.

Anyway, it’s a good start and I need to convince myself, just do it.
